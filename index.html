ABOUT ME

Better design, better experience

As a Mechanical Design Engineer at Aspire Industries, I specialised in crafting sheet metal structures using Advanced Solidworks, Ansys & AutoCAD to optimise efficiency. With a seamless track record of managing over 250 projects, I showcased exceptional organisational skills and effective project management capabilities.
By applying engineering principles and GD&T, I elevated structural integrity and performance, resulting in a 10% reduction in material and welding wastage. Additionally, I assumed the responsibilities of a Designated Production Manager, ensuring smooth communication with clients, coordinating with the design team, and maintaining vendor relations to uphold uninterrupted production processes.
My portfolio encompasses designing products for esteemed brands like Reliance Jio, Idea, Warpp, Raychem, Cadbury, Beardo, etc. Moreover, my proficiency in CNC Turret Punching and CNC Bending enables me to incorporate practical tolerances into designs and seamlessly integrate efficient sheet metal designs with CNC machining processes.
With hands-on experience overseeing the production process from start to finish and a solid foundation in mechanical design engineering, I am enthusiastic about leveraging my expertise in a new role as a Mechanical Design Engineer.
